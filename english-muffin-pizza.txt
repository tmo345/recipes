Ingredients:
- 2 english muffins
- 1 can of tomato sauce
- 6 ounces shredded mozzarella
- Oregano
- Salt
- Pepper

Directions:
- Set oven to broil at 500F
- Cut english muffins in half and toast for 2-3 minutes in toaster
- Lay toasted english muffins on cookie sheet
- Spoon tomato sauce on to each english muffin
- Add salt, pepper, and oregano according to preferences
- Top with shredded mozzarella
- Cook in oven until cheese melted and slightly browned on top (4-5 minutes)
